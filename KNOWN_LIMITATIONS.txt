ExoSnap Alpha — Known Limitations

PREREQUISITES
- Windows 11 (22H2 or later recommended)
- NVIDIA GPU with RTX-class NVENC support (RTX 20xx or later)
- NVIDIA Display Driver 536.23 or later
- Microsoft Visual C++ Redistributable 2022 x64
  Download: https://aka.ms/vs/17/release/vc_redist.x64.exe

KNOWN LIMITATIONS
- No installer; extract the ZIP and run exosnap.exe directly.
- No code signing; Windows SmartScreen may warn on first launch.
- MP4 output is visible but disabled; WebM is the supported alpha container.
- VideoPage and AudioPage are locked informational MVP pages.
- Video quality, FPS, and resolution settings are fixed for this alpha.
- Region capture is not available.
- Global hotkeys are unavailable in this build.
- Auto-split is not available.
- Mic preflight meter is active; SYS/APP audio meters activate when recording starts.
- Mic hot-plug is not automatic; use the Refresh button.
- Window capture can fail for minimized, cloaked, or very small windows.
- Settings persist locally in INI; format may change in later alpha builds.
- Stable display identity uses the GDI device name (e.g. \\.\DISPLAY1), which can be
  reassigned on topology change (monitor disconnect/reconnect or mode-set). A saved
  Region or Display target may point to a different physical monitor after a reboot
  or topology change; re-select the source manually in that case.
- Hot-swap during recording is not supported: disconnecting the configured capture
  device mid-session does not retarget the pipeline. Stop and restart the recording
  after reconnecting or selecting a new device.
