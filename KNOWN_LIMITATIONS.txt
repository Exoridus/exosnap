ExoSnap Alpha — Known Limitations

PREREQUISITES
- Windows 11 (22H2 or later recommended)
- NVIDIA GPU with RTX-class NVENC support (RTX 20xx or later)
- NVIDIA Display Driver 536.23 or later
- Microsoft Visual C++ Redistributable 2022 x64
  Download: https://aka.ms/vs/17/release/vc_redist.x64.exe

KNOWN LIMITATIONS
- No installer; extract the ZIP and run exosnap.exe directly.
- No code signing; Windows SmartScreen may warn on first launch.
- MP4 output is visible but disabled; MKV is the supported alpha container.
- VideoPage and AudioPage are locked informational MVP pages.
- Video quality, FPS, and resolution settings are fixed for this alpha.
- Region capture is not available.
- Global hotkeys are not registered in this build.
- Auto-split is not available.
- Audio meters show live activity during recording only, not before recording.
- Mic hot-plug is not automatic; use the Refresh button.
- Window capture can fail for minimized, cloaked, or very small windows.
- Settings are persisted locally in an INI file and may change in later alpha builds.
